Team Sirens

Luisa Goytia, Grace Turner, Jerry Wei, Melana Hammel

Project Idea: How to feel safer running or walking alone at night.

Population: Everyone who feels unsafe alone.

Motivation:

In any place where there are safe areas, you need to be smart. We want to create a

way for people to be able to feel safe being alone, either running or walking at night.

Process:

1. Researching runners and figuring out how they protect themselves.

2. Testing different solutions.

3. Testing different scenarios.
